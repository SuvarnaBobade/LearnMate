About the College

To promote the students who are needy, eligible and honest by helping them in giving hostel facilities and boosting their careers. To run educational institutions and make permanent provision for intelligent and professional students from the institutes. Boost and explore the students' intelligence, physical and mental development by supplying necessary facilities. Organizing cultural programs for the students to expose and increase their moral values. To promote the students in commercial, industrial, and technical fields of education. Establishing technical and mechanical background by arranging seminars and workshops for the students so they can earn and learn. Establishing an orphanage for the people who are orphans, handicapped, and widows. To get educational help from the institutions and by supplying necessary facilities to the above people.

To run institutions by taking the help of old-aged, orphans, handicapped, and honest people. To develop technical institutions and get necessary technical help from the other related institutions. To establish small-scale industries and get affiliation from the required institutions so as to encourage and develop interest in the people. To establish military educational institutions for spreading military education. To run public schools from college or military training points of view. To develop research counters for the poor and needy farmers. To do professional training for the development of technology, necessary facilities for research in computer science, laboratories.
